Wall Activities
Taha Tinana (Physical Wellbeing) Taking the dog for a walk everyday (even in the rain)
Taha Whānau (Family Wellbeing) Dedicating time each evening to catching up with family members to see how they are doing
Taha Hinengaro (Emotional and Mental Wellbeing) Starting each day with gratitude practice by naming 5 things I am grateful for.
Taha Wairua (Spiritual Wellbeing) Practicing silent meditation every evening to wind down
Whenua (Interconnections to the land and environment) Going along the beach at least twice a week with the dog
